monopleg10

A sculpted, one-handed keyboard with 10 keys. Designed for users with limited hand mobility. Ergonomically crafted for ease of use, with a compact layout that minimizes hand movement. Perfect for anyone needing a more accessible and efficient typing solution.

Comes preloaded with KMK firmware that transforms the 10 keys into a full fledged keyboard. Can be customized to your liking if instead it needs to be used for gaming or another specialized purpose.

Specifications

  • USB-C connection
  • Seeed Studio Xiao RP2040 microcontroller
  • Kailh Choc low profile switches
  • Sculpted keys for improved ergonomics
  • 3D printed with PLA
  • Handwired matrix circuit
  • Customizable firmware using python
